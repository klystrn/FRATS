ABOUT US

To add an extra layer of authentication using OpenCV and enhance security in companies, FRATS, the Facial Recognition Attendance Taking System, offers a contactless, scalable, and affordable AI-powered data collection solution. This system utilizes cameras and OpenCV to automatically identify and track individuals, making attendance taking more seamless, convenient, and automatic. FRATS caters to businesses of all sizes in various industries, including manufacturing, retail, healthcare, transportation, and institutions.
